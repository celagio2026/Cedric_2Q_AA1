Liquid Luck

As the name states, liquid luck was used in Hogwarts for the drinker to be extremely lucky after consuming just a few drops of it. Thus, this drink of ours is quite strong, yet tasty at the same time, explaining its small servings/sizes. Just one sip of it and it will take you to a new world where you will be filled with liberty and luck!

Main Ingredients:

vodka, lemon lime soda, lemonade, apple juice, luster dust, and simple syrup

Availabe in sizes:

small (20 ml)

large (45 ml)
