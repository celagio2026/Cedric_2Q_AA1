Polyjuice Potion

Polyjuice is one of the most difficult potions to brew in Hogwarts as with one sip, you can deliniate or be another person. Albeit we cannot replicate its canon recipe, we take our time in brewing every Polyjuice we make. It is a perfect refresher for sunny days with its fruity and fresh flavor that is truly rejuvenating and makes you feel like a new person!

Main Ingredients:

kiwi juice, lemon-lime soda, and apple sorbet

Availabe in sizes:

small (300 ml)

large (475 ml)
