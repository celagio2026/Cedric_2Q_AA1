Pumpkin Fizz

Featured in the Harry Potter books and movies, our Hogwarts-inspired pumpkin fizz tastes less of pumpkin than expected and more of an apple pie in liquid form with a hint of pumpkin flavor. It is perfect for halloween treats with its orange color and strong pumpkin taste.

Main ingredients:

apple, pumpkin, apricot, warm fall spices

Availabe in sizes:

small (100 ml)

large (150 ml)
