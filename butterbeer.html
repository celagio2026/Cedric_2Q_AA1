Butterbeer

Butterbeer is a non-alcoholic beverage reminiscent of shortbread and butterscotch. It makes its first appearance in the Harry Potter series in Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban when it is both discussed and later appears when Ron and Hermione go to Hogsmeade. This is a fan-favorite due to its unique flavors and a classic nostalgic drink all potterheads know.

Main Ingredients:

vanilla vodka, butterscotch schnapps

Availabe in sizes:

small (355 ml)

large (475 ml)
