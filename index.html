Indulge in the magical world of witchcraft and wizardry, embedded, blended, and incorporated in the liquid and aroma of our drinks. One sip and your whole world takes a leap.
Explore Our Wizarding World

Espresso Patronum is a Hogwarts-inspired cafe that offers drinks seen or read in the Harry Potter book and movie series. It takes these recipes and elevates them to a new lrvel so that one may really feel the Hogwarts vibe. Our brand name takes insipiration from a powerful spell in Harry Potter called 'Expecto Patronum.' This spell means I expect or await a guardian in Latin. To cast this spell is to vividly reminisce one's happiest memories. Thus, just like the spell, after entering Espresso Patronum, one is sure to engrave this experience in their memory as one of the happiest as our mission is to be your 'Expecto Patronum!'
